Spaghetti with Spicy Marinara

Description

Spaghetti served with marinara sauce spiced with garlic, crushed red pepper, and itallian seasonings.

w

Ingredients

  • 28 oz can crushed tomatos in puree
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 3 cloves pressed galic
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • enough olive oil to lightly coat the bottom of a mid size pan
  • 1 lb spaghetti

Directions

  1. Heat oil over medium heat.
  2. Add pressed garlic.
  3. Add crushed red pepper.
  4. Just before the garlic browns and the pepper burns, add the tomato puree.
  5. Add italian seasoning. Stir. Reduce heat to low.
  6. Bring water to a boil.
  7. Add spaghetti, stirring occasionally until cooked.
  8. Drain spaghetti.
  9. Serve with sauce.
